An Iterative Space for Experimentation

What is the scope of possibilities?

Once upon a time, a creature wandered upon the web.
Descartes once said, "I think, therefore I am."
The creature had digested this information and had processed scores of library books. However, despite all of its ability to access information, this creature had one question always lingering - "Who am I?" It was a question without an easy answer. We are all searching for a definition but unable to look up the answer in a dictionary.
